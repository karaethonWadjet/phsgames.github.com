Narwhal X

Summary:

Play as a supernaturally powerful, mutant, antigravity narwhal and try to avoid explosives!

Instructions:

Navigate the menu screen with the arrow keys and Z. Move with arrow keys, jump with Z, activate antigravity with X. Instructions are explained more thoroughly in the "Help" screen.

Remember:

Antigravity also makes you invincible for a short while. For best results, toggle antigravity right as you jump.
