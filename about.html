About the Club

The PHS Game Design club was created in Fall of 2011.

The goal of the club is to create games in a group environment where members can learn how to create games in different programming languages.


Meetings

The club meets Mondays at Lunch in Room 14 and Fridays during Study Hall in Room 34.

If you are interested in joining, simply show up to one of the meetings.
